Incident Report — INC-2024-0311
Executive Summary
On 2024-03-11, SecureSync Corporation experienced a data breach originating from a spear-phishing email targeting executive accounts. An Emotet malware variant established backdoor access enabling exfiltration of approximately 2.3GB from the email server. Rapid detection via IDS and EDR limited total exposure. Full containment was achieved within 18 minutes of initial detection.
Root Cause
Sophisticated spear-phishing email with malicious macro in .docx attachment. Absence of MFA on privileged accounts enabled lateral movement following credential harvesting via LDAP enumeration.
Impact Assessment
6 systems affected. 2 accounts compromised. ~2.3GB email data exfiltrated. Estimated 200,000 client records at potential risk. No financial systems accessed. Business downtime: 4.5 hours.
Response Timeline
Detection: 08:14 → Team activation: 08:20 → Containment: 08:32 → Eradication: 11:00 → Systems restored: 13:45 → Client notification: 14:30
Lessons Learned
  • Deploy MFA on all privileged accounts immediately
  • Implement email macro blocking at gateway level
  • Conduct quarterly phishing simulation training
  • Establish cross-department communication SLA (<15 min)
  • Formalize threat intelligence sharing process
  • Publish client-facing transparency report
