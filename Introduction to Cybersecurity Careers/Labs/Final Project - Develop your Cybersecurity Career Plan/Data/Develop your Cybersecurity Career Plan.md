# Final Project: Develop Your Cybersecurity Career Plan

## Overview

During this course, you have become familiar with various roles in Cybersecurity, as well as the skills, qualifications, and certifications required for the different roles. In this project, you will explore real Cybersecurity job postings, analyze them, and develop a plan to become job-ready.

**Estimated time needed:** 14 minutes

---

## Instructions

### Task 1: Identify a Cybersecurity Role and Find a Job Listing

#### [A] Select a Cybersecurity Job Role

First, identify a Cybersecurity job role that you want to pursue as a career. This may be a role you want to start your Cybersecurity career with or a role that you may aspire to take up at some future point in your career journey.

**Consider these common Cybersecurity roles:**

| Role Level               | Example Roles                                                                |
| :----------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Entry-Level**    | Cybersecurity Specialist, IT Auditor, Cybercrime Analyst, SOC Analyst        |
| **Mid-Level**      | Cybersecurity Analyst, Cybersecurity Consultant, Penetration Tester          |
| **Advanced-Level** | Cybersecurity Engineer, Cybersecurity Architect, Cybersecurity Manager, CISO |

#### [B] Search for Job Postings

Next, search for job postings related to the identified Cybersecurity role that you find appealing by going to a job board of your choice.

**Recommended Job Boards:**

| Job Board               | Website                                                     | Best For                                  |
| :---------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------- |
| **LinkedIn**      | [https://www.linkedin.com/jobs](https://www.linkedin.com/jobs) | Professional networking; company research |
| **Indeed**        | [https://www.indeed.com](https://www.indeed.com)               | Broad selection; easy filtering           |
| **Glassdoor**     | [https://www.glassdoor.com](https://www.glassdoor.com)         | Salary insights; company reviews          |
| **ZipRecruiter**  | [https://www.ziprecruiter.com](https://www.ziprecruiter.com)   | Matching technology; broad reach          |
| **USAJobs**       | [https://www.usajobs.gov](https://www.usajobs.gov)             | US government positions                   |
| **JobBank.gc.ca** | [https://www.jobbank.gc.ca](https://www.jobbank.gc.ca)         | Canadian positions                        |

**Filtering Options:**

- **Location(s):** Local, remote, specific cities/countries
- **Employment Type:** Full-time, part-time, contract, consulting
- **Industry:** Banking, Healthcare, IT, Retail, Government, Education
- **Experience Level:** Entry-level, mid-level, senior

Identify **one job listing** that interests you and that you can see yourself applying for when you have the right skills and qualifications.

### Task 2: Understand Job Details and Requirements

Review the identified job listing thoroughly and document the following details:

#### Job Details

| Category                  | Information to Document                    |
| :------------------------ | :----------------------------------------- |
| **Job Title**       | Exact title as listed                      |
| **Company Name**    | Organization offering the position         |
| **Job Location**    | City, state, country; remote/hybrid/onsite |
| **Employment Type** | Full-time, part-time, contract             |
| **Salary Range**    | If listed                                  |
| **Benefits**        | If listed (healthcare, 401k, etc.)         |
| **Job Description** | Summary of role responsibilities           |

#### Job Requirements

| Category                       | Information to Document                                |
| :----------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Technical Skills**     | Specific IT and cybersecurity skills required          |
| **Soft Skills**          | Communication, teamwork, problem-solving, etc.         |
| **Education**            | Minimum degree requirements; preferred fields of study |
| **Experience**           | Years of experience required; type of experience       |
| **Certifications**       | Required or preferred certifications                   |
| **Other Qualifications** | Clearance requirements, language skills, etc.          |

---

### Task 3: Determine Job Attractiveness

Identify aspects of the job posting that you find suitable and those you don't find appropriate.

#### Evaluation Questions

| Aspect                     | Your Assessment                                       |
| :------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Location**         | Is the location acceptable? Would you relocate?       |
| **Salary**           | Does the salary range meet your expectations?         |
| **Responsibilities** | Do the daily tasks interest you?                      |
| **Company/Industry** | Is this an organization/industry you want to work in? |
| **Growth Potential** | Does this role lead to advancement opportunities?     |
| **Requirements**     | Are the requirements realistic and achievable?        |

**Decision:** Are you still interested in applying for a job like this in the future?

- If **YES**, proceed to Task 4.
- If **NO**, repeat Tasks 1-3 until you find a Cybersecurity job you would like to pursue.

---

### Task 4: Identify Gaps in Your Portfolio

Perform a self-assessment and create an inventory of your current portfolio. Compare this with the job requirements to identify gaps.

#### Self-Assessment Template

| Category                   | Your Current Status                              | Job Requirements                  | Gap Analysis                                |
| :------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------ |
| **Education**        | List your degrees, diplomas, or current programs | Required/preferred education      | What additional education do you need?      |
| **Technical Skills** | List skills you already possess                  | Required technical skills         | Which skills are missing?                   |
| **Soft Skills**      | List your soft skills                            | Required soft skills              | Which soft skills need development?         |
| **Experience**       | Years and type of experience                     | Required experience               | How much more experience needed? What type? |
| **Certifications**   | Certifications you hold                          | Required/preferred certifications | Which certifications are missing?           |
| **Other**            | Other relevant qualifications                    | Other requirements                | What else is missing?                       |

#### Example Gap Analysis

| Category                   | Current Status                  | Job Requirement                            | Gap                                                     |
| :------------------------- | :------------------------------ | :----------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Education**        | Bachelor's in Business          | Bachelor's in CS or related                | No gap (degree accepted)                                |
| **Technical Skills** | Basic networking, Windows admin | SIEM tools, vulnerability scanning, Python | Missing SIEM experience, Python, vulnerability scanning |
| **Soft Skills**      | Teamwork, communication         | Communication, problem-solving             | Need to develop problem-solving examples                |
| **Experience**       | 1 year IT support               | 2-3 years security experience              | Need 1-2 more years security experience                 |
| **Certifications**   | Security+                       | CISSP preferred                            | Need to pursue CISSP                                    |
